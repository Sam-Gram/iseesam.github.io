Plot Summary
Ben the Ant and Bill the Bee play a game of Hide-and-Seek. Bill hides first in a net,then Ben hides in a bus. Later Ben hides in a bed. When Bill looks for Ben in a honey jar, he gets stuck inside. He spies Ben nearby in a bed and calls for help. Ben rescues Bill with a net and together they enjoy spilled honey from the jar.
