Plot Summary
Sam has a surfboard. He has fun riding the waves. He lets Mit and Mat have turns on his board. Mat falls off the board and disappears in the water. When Sam and Mit see Mat's hat and bell drift ashore, they fear Mat is lost. Mat is safe, however, but decides that the sun is better than surf.
