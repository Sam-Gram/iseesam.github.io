Plot Summary
Mit and Sam find a shell at the beach. Mit sets up a stand and tries to sell the shell. Angry at Mit, Sam fills a bag with sand to sell, and sets up a stand right next to Mit's. Both Sam and Mit try to sell their wares to Mat, but he refuses to buy. Instead the mischievous rat tricks them with some apples and while they are eating, he takes the shell and sand for himself.
