Plot Summary
Ann is taking a walk in the park. When it begins to rain, she puts an umbrella over her head and runs. Soon Ann meets Mat who asks to share her umbrella. As they run, Mit and Sam join them. The small umbrella falls over Ann's head when Will tries to squeeze under it. The animals run for cover elsewhere, as Ann angrily pokes her head through the top of her torn umbrella. The sun comes out, and the animals play happily together in the sunshine.
