Plot Summary
Ann, Sis, Mit, Sam, and Mat go for a boat ride together. After a while, each one begins to lose interest in the ride and finds a better place to sit and in the boat. Only Ann the Giraffe is left in the boat. She wonders where she might find a new place to sit with a friend. Finally, a lemonade-sipping alligator appears and invites Ann to join him in the water. Now Ann is happy, because she has found a comfortable place to sit with a friend.
