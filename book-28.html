Plot Summary
Ed has a net and a sheet which he and Baby Bear convert into a tent. They sit under the sheet and then fill their makeshift tent with toys and food. The other animals bring more food and want to crowd into the tent. Ed tries to shut the tent but it collapses. Baby Bear, Mat, Nan, and Mit pick up the food and continue the picnic. Ed picks up the net and sheet and uses them to take a nap.
