Plot Summary
Ann the Giraffe struts through the forest in her new outfit which includes a fancy hat with a long-stemmed flower on it. Ann sits down under a tree a falls asleep. Mit the Chimp and Nan the Parrot meet in the same tree, see the flower, and discover the hat. They are unaware that the hat belongs to Ann who remains asleep below. When Ann awakens, she is startled to see the parrot in her hat. Ann finally gets her hat back intact and all the animals nap under the tree.
