Plot Summary
Ed the Dog and Nell the Bird undertake a joint project. Ed has a basket which he wants to fill with cherries. He cannot climb up the cherry tree and Nell cannot reach up to any of the cherries. Finally, Nell stands on top of Ed's back and they fill the basket this way. When they go home, they work together and make three big cherry pies. All the animals stop by and are full of praise for Nell and Ed as a team.
