Plot Summary
Mat settles down for an afternoon in the sun with a book and some refreshments. Sis joins him and they both doze off. While they are sleeping, uninvited guests arrive. The ants would like to picnic too! Mat and Sis wake up in time to stop the ants from taking their food. Then they invite the ants to join them for some cool lemonade.
