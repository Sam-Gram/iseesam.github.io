Plot Summary
Mama Kangaroo falls asleep during a sun bath and Nat scampers away. He meets Mit who offers him a ride in his fancy open car. A rain cloud appears and rain begins to fall. The rain drenches Mit and Nat, and awakens Mama Kangaroo. The rain is brief; the sun shines again and Mit takes Nat and his Mama Kangaroo for a ride.
