Plot Summary
Mit the Chimp makes a paper-bag lion mask and puts it over his head. He pretends to be Sam the Lion and teases the other animals. Sis, Nan, and a new character, Baby Bear, see through Mit's disguise and are angry at his tricks. Sam himself is the angriest when he discovers Mit's impersonation. Finally Mit drops his lion mask and admits to all the animals that Sam is the real lion.
