Plot Summary
Will's sheet is muddy so he hangs it on the line. He beats the sheet with his trunk and tries to get the dirt out. Ann and Sam help him, but the sheet falls down on all of them. Mit joins them and they decide to wash the sheet in the well. The sheet slips into the well and falls on a fish. When the animals finally get the sheet out, they wring it and hang it up to dry. When the sheet has dried, the animals have fun tossing Will up and down on it.
