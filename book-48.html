Plot Summary
A friendly "fun fish" plays a silly prank on Mat while he is on the beach. Mat runs after him, but the fun fish gets away. Sam and Mit do not believe Mat when he tells them about the fun fish. When they do see him, Sam and Mit play a game of tag with him. The fun fish gets away by jumping into the water, leaving Mat's bell behind on the shore.
