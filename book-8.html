Plot Summary
Mat, Mit, and Sis are playing ball on a very hot day. They suddenly see a shaded place and decide to play where it is not so hot. Then they discover that the shade is really the shadow of Will the Elephant. As the sun goes down, off they all go for a ride on Will's back.
