Plot Summary
The animals are playing school. Mat, as the teacher, is trying to teach Sam, Mit, and Sis how to read their names. He starts by writing Sis's name in the sand. The animals have some difficulty reading the name and Mat finally tells them what it is. Sis is delighted and reads her name for the others. Mat then writes the other names, including his own, and the animals all have fun reading.
