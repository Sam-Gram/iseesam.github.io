Plot Summary
Mat the Rat gets ready to go to the zoo and takes fish and peanuts along for the animals. While he feeds the seal, a baby elephant tries to reach for a peanut. Mat begins to tease the baby elephant. Mama Elephant is angry. She takes the nuts away from Mat. But Mat soon becomes friends with Mama Elephant and her baby.
