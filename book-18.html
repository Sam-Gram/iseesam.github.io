Plot Summary
One by one the animals climb up to sit in their tree house. When Sid the Horse tries to join them, however, he is too heavy and the tree house falls with everyone in it. Sid is blamed for the resulting mess so he undertakes to repair the tree house. All of the animals get back in the tree house with Sid solidly supporting them.
