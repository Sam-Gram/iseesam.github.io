Plot Summary
Mit has fun running with a special pair of stilts which have "feet" on them. When Mit sees Sam approaching in the distance, he runs and climbs a tree to hide. The big feet on the stilts hang down from the branches of the tree and frighten Sam and Sis. The stilts slip and Mit falls from the tree. Then all three animals have fun running and jumping.
